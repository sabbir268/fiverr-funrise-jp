HOW J.P FUNDING LLC WORKS FOR YOU

Cash Advances are developed for plaintiffs who have a suit that is pending and need cash to help them until their suit settles. The money which you borrow is provided at zero risk to you. What that means is if your situation does not reach a positive outcome, you will not have to repay us. We’ve made the application process simple because we know you are going through difficult times and you need some help. Several cases may qualify, including employment cases, personal injury and many more.

Remember – we do all of the work: we will get in contact with the law firm and lawyers which are handling your current case. We will collect all the documents to produce a proper assessment regarding the potential and viability value of your case. We make a choice to provide the advance to you, in some cases within 24 hours on approval. We transfer the funds to you when your advance is approved by us. We are here for you to make this as easy as possible so you and your family can rest easy.

IT’S EASY – THERE IS NO CREDIT CHECK, NO FEES, AND NO PAYBACK UNLESS YOU WIN!

Your cash advance is provided on a non-recourse basis, meaning that your advance does not need to be re-paid if your situation is lost for whatever reason. J.P Legal Funding LLC doesn’t charge any upfront fees. It’s free to apply and there’s no fee to review your application.

Your cash advance is only re-paid when you obtain money on your case. We’ve developed this policy because we know you are going through tough financial times and that you cannot afford to take more financial risks. Consequently, we protect you from further risk by giving you the funds you need before your case is settled. We’ve made the application process extremely easy. Your friendly case review professional will only ask a couple of simple questions to determine whether you qualify.
